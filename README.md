# IIES-App
The IIES is a ln Islamic tarbiyah support app for Islamic eating science students. The repository for all files needed to build the IIES App.

The first batch of files is what was downloaded from a free online website design at the Replit website done in the first week of July 2026 in the Gregorian calendar.

The plan is to use github, codespaces, and gitpages to actually deploy the web app soon, God willing.

---------------

A little information aboot the app.

Islamic eating science requires serious Islamic personal development.

That is called التربية الشكصية الإسلامية .

It is obtained through patience and had the need of true Islamic recitation of the Qur'an (التلاوة), comprehension (الفهيم), and contemplation (التدبّر).

Glory be to God.
 
The app utilises the technology of a modern internet app to assist Muslims to achieve true focus and progress in their Islamic eating science study.
 
It turns the users towards Almighty God by engaging them in Islamic study techniques and the culture of learning through a dialogic process.
 
Some of the understanding derives from the 'Tarbiyah Shaksiyah' methodology notes written by The Shaksiyah foundation in the UK.
 
The flourishing of the divine nature within (الفطرة), along with understanding the Islamic eating science modules themselves is the mark of success.
 
The app directs the students to the modules, all of which draw on the Qur'an.
 
There are five screens. They each carry a simple data layer.
 
1. Dashboard
2. Courses
3. Notes
4. Contacts
5. Teacher

The Courses screen includes a further data layer: the lessons.
 
There is also a link from the dashboard to the digital lobby of the IIES website.
 
The other screens do not contain links, only displays and forms, so that student focus remains within their studies.
 
There is a single nav controller: a horizontal nav bar at the top on mobile or side bar on desktop.
 
At this stage, there is no auth for basic users.

🟧 Note that the app is not yet published to any site at 8-July-2026.
 
Authorisation is only required for a teacher when a student's understanding is to be confirmed in a lesson record by the teacher.
